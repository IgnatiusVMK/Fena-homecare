Welcome to Fena Home Care

We clean homes, office buildings, gardens and roofs.

We are a cleaning agency that is aimed at touching every part of your space to make sure you will have a sparkling clean space. From our slogan “We leave Footprints”, you will have us in mind the next time you want to do you cleaning.

We specialize in delivering exceptional cleaning and care services tailored to your needs. From homes to commercial spaces, we ensure spotless results with honesty, transparency, and professionalism. Entrust us with your spaces and experience excellence. Together, let’s create cleaner, brighter environments for a better future.
